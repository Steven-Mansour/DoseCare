Privacy Policy

At DoseCare, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our pill dispenser application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details.
  • Health Data: Medication schedules, dosage, and adherence tracking.
  • Device Information: Usage patterns and technical data from the pill dispenser.
  • Communication Data: Messages between users and caregivers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • 💊 To send medication reminders and notifications.
  • 📡 To synchronize data with your smart pill dispenser.
  • 🔐 To improve security and ensure authorized access.
  • 📊 To analyze usage trends and enhance app performance.

3. Data Security

We implement strong security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your personal and health data from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing and Third-Party Access

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • 👨‍⚕️ Caregivers & Family: If you choose to share access with them.
  • 🏥 Healthcare Providers: When authorized for medical support.
  • 🔐 Legal Compliance: If required by law to protect your safety or comply with regulations.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • 📥 Request a copy of your personal information.
  • 🛑 Opt-out of certain data collection or notifications.
  • 🗑️ Delete your account and associated data upon request.

6. Updates to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements in our services. Any changes will be posted here, and we encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

📧 Email: support@DoseCare.com
